Objetivos

  • Introducir el tema: Redes sociales y riesgos
  • Activar conocimientos previos
  • Comprender ideas globales de un texto

Debate

Debate: Do you use social media?
Work in pairs or small groups

  • What risks appear in the video?
  • What should you NOT do online?
  • What must you do to stay safe?
  • Have you ever received a scam message?

Metodología:

  • Active Learning

Lluvia de ideas

Brainstorming: What risks can you find on social media?

Examples: - Fake accounts

                   - Scams

                   - Sharing personal information

Lectura

Reading: A Story About Online Risks

Laura is a young woman who uses social media every day. She enjoys sharing photos, chatting with friends, and following her favorite influencers.

One day, she receives a message from a stranger. The message looks friendly and includes a link. The person says it is something interesting and asks her to click on it.

Laura is curious, so she clicks on the link. Suddenly, something strange happens. She cannot access her account anymore. Her password does not work.

Later, she discovers that her account has been hacked. The stranger was a scammer.

Now Laura understands an important lesson: she must be careful online. She should not click on unknown links, and she must protect her personal information.

Comprehension Questions:

  1. Who is Laura?
  2. What message does she receive?
  3. What happens when she clicks the link?
  4. What lesson does she learn?
